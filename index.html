Why certified matters

Not all batteries are equal.

The market is flooded with cheap imports. Here's why certification isn't optional — it's the difference between a safe investment and a liability.

UL-9540 Certification

Most direct-import systems lack UL-9540 fire safety ratings. Without this, your local utility won't approve grid interconnection — and your installation is technically illegal in Texas.

Insurance Compliance

Installing non-certified lithium systems can void your homeowner's insurance entirely. One thermal event with zero coverage — that's a risk no homeowner should take. We install only certified systems.

True Grid Compatibility

Overseas units are built for different grid standards. US 120V/240V split-phase power permanently damages motors in A/C units, well pumps, and major appliances. Our systems are built for Texas grids.

Why San Antonio homeowners choose us

Eight reasons your battery
pays for itself.

Every benefit below is specific to CPS Energy territory — not generic solar marketing.

01

Whole-Home Backup Power

Guards against San Antonio's frequent thunderstorms and summer storms. 13.5 kWh usable / 11.5 kW continuous keeps your AC, fridge, lights, Wi-Fi, and medical devices running — no flicker, seconds to switch.

02

Summer Bill Savings

Beats the CPS Peak Capacity Charge — an extra ~2.06¢/kWh on all usage over 600 kWh from June–September. The Powerwall auto-discharges during 3–7 PM peak hours, cutting your hottest summer bills.

03

Earn Cash from CPS Energy

Because you own the hardware, you can enroll in CPS Energy's My Battery Rewards program. Earn a $10 bill credit every time CPS dispatches your battery during grid stress — year-round.

04

Maximize Solar Value

CPS pays only ~3–4¢/kWh for exported solar, but charges ~12.5¢/kWh to buy it back. Store it instead. Self-consume 100% of your solar — and the battery still charges during a blackout.

05

Rate Increase Protection

CPS average rates are ~12.5–13.6¢/kWh with potential 2026 hikes from grid growth. Storm Watch auto-charges your battery before bad weather. Lock in cheaper energy and shield your family from future bill shocks.

06

Safety & Peace of Mind

No food spoilage. No heat stress during Texas summers. Quiet, fuel-free operation with real-time app monitoring and unlimited cycles. Reliable power for CPAP machines, medications, and home offices.

07

Increased Home Value

Backup power is a top-tier upgrade in storm-prone Texas. The sleek Tesla Powerwall 3 integrates cleanly into any home. Buyers increasingly seek energy-independent homes — this system boosts your resale value.

08

Faster Payback

Stack the 30% Federal ITC, CPS My Battery Rewards credits, and peak-hour savings. Most customers reach payback in 7–12 years — far better ROI than a traditional generator with ongoing fuel and maintenance costs.

"Do I need solar to add a battery?"
No. You can add a battery to your existing solar system, or build solar + battery together from scratch. Grid-tied means you're still connected to CPS — the battery just optimizes your usage and gives you backup when the grid goes down.

Your battery backs up the circuits we design with you — full home or essentials, your call. Grid-tied means you use the grid when you want, battery when you need it. The best of both worlds.
